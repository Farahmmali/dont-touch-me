About the Project

The Trash Bin That Opens for You

This project introduces a smart trash bin that opens automatically without physical contact. It is designed to improve hygiene, reduce germ transmission, and provide a more convenient waste disposal experience.

  • Automatic lid opening using a sensor-based mechanism
  • No physical contact required during use
  • Helps reduce the spread of germs
  • Suitable for both household and public environments

How It Works

Three Simple Smart Steps

The system combines sensing, processing, and automatic movement to deliver a clean touch-free experience.

1. Detect

The sensor detects the presence of a hand near the lid.

2. Process

The controller receives the signal and decides when to open the bin.

3. Open

The lid opens automatically, allowing touch-free waste disposal.
